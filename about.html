Our Story

Building digital solutions for local businesses across coastal Karnataka

Webotrex is a professional web design and development agency based in Kundapura, Udupi District, Karnataka. We specialize in creating custom HTML websites, modern web designs, and complete digital solutions for local businesses including cafes, bakeries, startups, shops, and service providers across coastal Karnataka.

Unlike template-based solutions, we build fast-loading, SEO-friendly websites using clean HTML, CSS, and JavaScript without WordPress dependency. Our custom-coded approach ensures superior performance, security, and flexibility tailored to your business needs.

With experience serving businesses in Kundapura, Udupi, Mangalore, and surrounding areas, we understand the local market and provide direct communication, on-time delivery, and long-term support. Our affordable pricing makes professional web presence accessible to small businesses looking to grow online.
